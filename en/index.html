About the App

What is YMP TV Screensaver?

YMP TV Screensaver is an application that provides users with the ability to use unique screensavers and images on their devices.

Built-in Screensavers

The app comes with 5 pre-installed active screensavers and images that can be used on your desktop.

Additional Widgets

In addition, screensavers can display additional widgets: weather widget, clock widget, and others.

Ease of Use

The application provides users with the ability to quickly and easily install and change screensavers.
